# Clean Claim Logic

Simple explanation of what a clean claim indicator usually means.

- **clean_claim_ind = 1**: Claim passed basic format and required-field checks on first receipt.
- **clean_claim_ind = 0**: Claim needed extra handling, had exceptions, or failed one or more intake or validation checks.
- **Note**: A clean claim can still later deny, pend, or adjust based on deeper adjudication rules.
