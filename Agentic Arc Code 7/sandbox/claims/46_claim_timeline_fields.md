# Claim Timeline Fields

Simple explanation of the main dates in a claim.

- **svc_from_date**: Date service started.
- **svc_to_date**: Date service ended.
- **received_date**: Date claim was received.
- **finalized_date**: Date claim decision was finalized.
- **paid_date**: Date payment was issued.
- **event_ts**: Timestamp of a claim lifecycle event.
- **tat_days**: Total turnaround time in days from receipt to final decision.
