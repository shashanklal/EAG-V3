# Auto vs Manual Adjudication

Simple explanation of how claims may be processed.

- **auto_adjudicated_ind = 1**: Claim was processed mainly by rules with little or no human touch.
- **auto_adjudicated_ind = 0**: Claim required manual review or examiner action.
- **Auto adjudication**: Usually faster for standard claims that pass rules and edits.
- **Manual adjudication**: Usually used for complex, exception, or policy-sensitive claims.
