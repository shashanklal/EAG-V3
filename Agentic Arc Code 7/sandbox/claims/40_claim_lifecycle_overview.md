# Claim Lifecycle Overview

High-level stages that a US health claim may pass through.

- **Claim creation**: Provider or member builds the claim with services, dates, and billing details.
- **Intake**: Payer receives the claim and performs format, member, and provider checks.
- **Validation and edits**: System runs eligibility, coding, duplicate, and policy edits.
- **Pend or rework**: Claim waits for missing information, correction, or further review.
- **Adjudication**: Rules engine or examiner determines allowed amount, payment, or denial.
- **Notification**: Provider and member receive remittance or denial information.
- **Payment**: Approved payment is issued to the provider or member.
- **Appeal or adjustment**: Claim can be reopened for appeal, correction, or post-payment review.
