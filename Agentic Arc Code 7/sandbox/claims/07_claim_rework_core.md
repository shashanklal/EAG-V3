# Claim Rework - Core Rework Fields

Fields created when a claim is reopened, corrected, appealed, or otherwise reworked.

## rework_ind
Synthetic flag showing that claim entered rework after initial processing.

## rework_reason
Reason the claim was sent back for more work.

## rework_source
Who triggered rework, such as provider, member, system edit, appeal team, or auditor.

## rework_received_date
Date rework request or missing information was received.

## info_request_date
Date payer asked for more information.

## info_received_date
Date additional information was received.

## appeal_ind
Flag showing whether a formal appeal was filed.

## appeal_level
Synthetic level of appeal such as first-level or second-level.
