# Claim Adjudication - Controls, Reviews, and Timing

Review, policy, control, and timing fields commonly used during adjudication.

## edits_passed
Synthetic list of edits passed, such as eligibility, coding, and duplicate edits.

## fraud_check_status
Synthetic result of fraud, waste, and abuse screening.

## medical_necessity_status
Synthetic outcome of medical necessity review.

## authorization_status
Synthetic result of prior authorization validation.

## coordination_of_benefits_ind
Synthetic flag showing whether another payer must be considered first.

## benefit_applied
Synthetic description of deductible, copay, coinsurance, or non-covered benefit handling.

## adjudication_queue
Work queue or rules path used for the claim review.

## adjudicator_id
Synthetic ID of the claims examiner for manual review.

## finalized_date
Date final adjudication decision was completed.

## paid_date
Date payment was issued if payment was made.

## tat_days
Turnaround time in days from receipt to finalization.
