# Claim Financial Amounts Dictionary

Simple explanation of common claim financial terms.

- **charge_amt**: Amount billed by the provider for one service line.
- **allowed_amt**: Amount the plan allows before payment and member cost share are applied.
- **paid_amt**: Amount actually paid after rules and member responsibility are applied.
- **total_charge_amt**: Sum of all provider billed amounts on the claim.
- **total_allowed_amt**: Sum of all allowed amounts on the claim.
- **total_paid_amt**: Sum of all payments made on the claim.
