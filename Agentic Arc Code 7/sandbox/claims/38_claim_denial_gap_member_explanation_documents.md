# Claim Denial Gap Guidance - Documents That May Be Needed

Synthetic document gaps that can be used to explain what is still needed.

- **Medical records**: Clinical notes or chart pages supporting why the service was needed.
- **Itemized bill**: Detailed provider bill showing each service and charge.
- **Referral form**: Referral document from the treating or primary provider.
- **Authorization letter**: Proof that the service was approved before treatment.
- **Coordination of benefits form**: Information about other insurance when more than one payer exists.
- **Accident details**: Information needed when injuries may involve another liable party.
- **Corrected claim form**: A corrected submission fixing missing or wrong fields.
- **Member statement**: Additional explanation from the member when requested.
