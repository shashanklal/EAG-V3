# Claim Rework - Operational Tracking Fields

Operational tracking fields used during rework handling and follow-up.

## call_count
Synthetic number of calls received about the claim.

## contact_channel
Synthetic communication channel such as call, portal, fax, or mail.

## reopen_date
Synthetic date when claim was reopened for rework.

## revalidated_date
Date claim passed validation again after new information was received.

## rework_owner_team
Synthetic team currently handling the rework.

## rework_sla_days
Synthetic SLA target for rework completion.

## final_status_after_rework
Final status after rework is completed.

## root_cause_category
Synthetic root cause bucket such as missing info, coverage issue, coding issue, or duplicate.
