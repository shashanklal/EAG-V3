# Claim Status Dictionary

Simple meanings of claim and line statuses found in the data.

- **Received**: Claim has been accepted into the processor system.
- **Validated**: Basic edits or checks have been completed.
- **InReview**: Claim is in a queue for manual or rules review.
- **Pended**: Claim is waiting for missing information or a decision.
- **Paid**: Claim or line has been approved and paid.
- **Denied**: Claim or line has been rejected with no payment.
- **PartiallyDenied**: Some services were paid and some were denied or reduced.
- **Adjusted**: An earlier decision or amount was corrected or recalculated.
- **Closed**: Processing cycle for the claim has been completed.
- **Rework**: Claim returned for correction or additional handling.
