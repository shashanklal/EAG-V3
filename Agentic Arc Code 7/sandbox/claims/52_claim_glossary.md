# Claim Glossary

Short glossary of common US healthcare claim terms.

- **Adjudication**: The process of deciding how a claim should be paid, denied, or adjusted.
- **Allowed amount**: The amount a plan recognizes for payment purposes.
- **Appeal**: A request to review a denial or payment decision again.
- **Clean claim**: A claim with required information that passes basic edits.
- **Coordination of benefits**: Rules used when more than one health plan may pay.
- **Deductible**: Amount the member must pay before certain benefits start.
- **EDI**: Electronic Data Interchange used to send claims electronically.
- **Pend**: A temporary hold while more information or review is needed.
- **Remittance**: Payment explanation sent to a provider after claim processing.
- **Utilization management**: Processes that check medical necessity and authorization rules.
