# Claim Denial Gap Guidance - Core Missing Information Patterns

Synthetic guidance to help explain common denial or pend causes to a member in simple English.

- **Missing member ID or coverage mismatch**: Your claim could not be matched clearly to your health plan enrollment.
- **Missing provider credentials**: The claim did not have enough provider details to confirm who gave the service.
- **Missing diagnosis code**: The medical reason for the service was not included or was not valid.
- **Missing procedure details**: The service code, units, or billed details were incomplete.
- **Missing authorization**: The service may have required approval before care was given.
- **Missing referral**: A referral from your primary doctor may have been required.
- **Duplicate submission**: The same claim or service appears to have been sent more than once.
- **Coverage limitation**: The service may not be covered under the member plan or benefit package.
