# Claim Intake - Matching, Exceptions, and Attachments

Additional intake fields used for matching, exception handling, and document intake.

## duplicate_check_ind
Synthetic flag showing whether duplicate screening was run at intake.

## member_match_status
Synthetic result of matching the submitted member details to enrollment records.

## provider_match_status
Synthetic result of matching provider details to provider master data.

## intake_exception_code
Synthetic code for intake edit failures such as missing field or invalid format.

## intake_exception_desc
Plain-English explanation of the intake issue.

## attachment_count
Synthetic count of documents received with the claim.

## document_type
Synthetic type of attachment such as medical record, referral, or invoice.
