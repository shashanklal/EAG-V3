# Claim Create Features - Line, Coding, and Amount Fields

Simple descriptions of line-level details and billing amounts captured during claim creation.

## pos_code
Place of service code that tells where care was given.

## dx_code
Diagnosis code that explains the medical reason for the service.

## units
Number of services, visits, or quantity billed.

## charge_amt
Amount billed by the provider for a line.

## total_charge_amt
Total billed amount for the full claim.

## network_ind
Shows whether provider is in-network or out-of-network.

## lob
Line of business such as Commercial, Medicare, or Medicaid.

## region
Geographic market or service region.

## platform
Processing platform or claims engine used for the claim.

## funding_arrangement
Shows whether plan is Fully Insured or ASO/self-funded.

## submission_channel
How the claim is submitted, such as EDI, Portal, or Paper.
