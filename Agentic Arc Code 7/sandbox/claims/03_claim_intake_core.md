# Claim Intake - Core Intake Fields

Fields created or stamped by the claim processing company when a claim is received.

## received_date
Date the claim was received by the payer or claims processor.

## submission_channel
Channel used to submit the claim, such as EDI, Portal, or Paper.

## clean_claim_ind
Flag showing whether the claim passed basic intake edits as a clean claim.

## received_amount
Amount recognized at intake for the claim or batch. In this synthetic pack, use total billed amount as the intake amount.

## intake_batch_id
Synthetic batch ID used to group claims received together.

## source_file_name
Synthetic source file or image name linked to intake submission.

## received_time
Synthetic timestamp showing when intake logged the claim.

## ocr_confidence
Synthetic confidence score when paper claims are scanned.
