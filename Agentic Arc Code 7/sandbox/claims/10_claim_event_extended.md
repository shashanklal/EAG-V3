# Claim Event - Extended Event Attributes

Additional event attributes useful for reasoning over timelines and operations.

## event_category
Synthetic higher-level category such as Intake, Review, Rework, Adjudication, Payment, or Notification.

## event_outcome
Synthetic simple outcome label such as success, hold, denial, paid, or update.

## event_sla_bucket
Synthetic SLA bucket such as same day, 1-3 days, 4-7 days, or over 7 days.
