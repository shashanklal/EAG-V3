# Claim Denial Prevention Tips

Simple tips to reduce common denials and pends.

- **Verify eligibility early**: Confirm member coverage before or at the time of service.
- **Submit complete coding**: Include valid diagnosis, procedure, POS, and revenue codes.
- **Check authorization rules**: Make sure required approvals are in place before billing.
- **Avoid duplicates**: Confirm the claim was not submitted already before resending.
- **Attach needed documents**: Send medical records, referrals, and corrected forms when required.
- **Validate provider data**: Ensure rendering and billing provider information is correct.
- **Review member cost share**: Explain deductible or coinsurance expectations early.
- **Use corrected claim indicators**: When resubmitting, clearly mark the bill as corrected or replacement.
