# Claim Denial Reason Playbook

Simple member-friendly explanation patterns for denial codes seen in the sample data.

- **CO-16**: The claim is missing information or has something invalid that must be corrected.
- **CO-18**: The service looks like it was already billed before, so it may be a duplicate.
- **CO-50**: The service may not meet medical necessity rules based on plan policy.
- **CO-97**: The service may not be covered by the member benefit plan.
- **CO-151**: The claim amount was adjusted rather than fully paid at the billed amount.
- **PR-1**: Part of the cost is member responsibility, such as deductible or cost share.
